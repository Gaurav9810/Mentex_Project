Frequently Asked Questions

Mental HealthMate is an online platform dedicated to promoting mental well-being. It provides users with access to various resources, including self-help tools, professional consultations, and real-time emergency support for mental health crises. Our goal is to offer a safe and accessible space for individuals to seek assistance and improve their mental health.

Booking an appointment is simple and convenient. Visit the "Appointments" section on our website, choose a licensed mental health professional from the list, and select a time that suits your schedule. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details. Additionally, our platform supports video consultations for your convenience and comfort.
